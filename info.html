I believe that technology should connect people

...so I build interfaces that embody accessibility, usability, and empathy.

I currently work as a UX Engineer at Designlab, an online design education platform. I combine my expertise in User Experience and Front End Development to serve as a cross-functional collaborator and liaison between designers and engineers.

Over the past five years, I have been researching how crafting and tech overlap: I dove into the interwoven histories of these practices, created technology for crafters, and used craft as a pathway to learning engineering and programming skills. Read more about my personal projects and inspiration.

Currently keeping my plants alive, my bike tires full, and my cats well-fed in sunny St. Pete, Florida. To contact me, send an email to hello@ezralamb.com.
