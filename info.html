I am a developer, designer, and crafter living in Saint Petersburg, Florida.

I believe that technology should connect people, so I build digital and physical interfaces that embody accessibility, usability, and empathy.

Over the past five years, I have been researching how crafting and tech overlap: I have delved into the interwoven histories of these practices, created technology for crafters, and used craft as a pathway to learning engineering and programming skills.

I currently work as a Front End Developer at Designlab, an online design education platform.

To contact me, send an email to hello@ezralamb.com.
