Hi! I'm Ezra.
I believe that technology should connect people, so I build interfaces that embody accessibility, usability, and empathy.

I am a User Experience Designer and Front End Developer. I use my hybrid expertise to serve as a cross-functional collaborator and liaison between designers and engineers.

Over the past several years I have also been researching how crafting and tech overlap: I dove into the interwoven histories of these practices, created technology for crafters, and used craft as a pathway to learning engineering and programming skills. Read more about my personal projects and inspiration.

Currently keeping my plants alive, my bike tires full, and my cats well-fed in sunny Palm Harbor, Florida. To contact me, send an email to hello@ezralamb.com.
